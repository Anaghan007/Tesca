Global Visa Pathways

Authentic consultancy with expert case officers for smooth immigration.

STUDENT VISA

Benefits:

  • • Part-time work rights (20 hrs/week)
  • • Post-study work permit up to 3 years
  • • World-class degree recognition

Requirements:

  • • Offer letter from DLI/University
  • • IELTS/PTE Proof (6.0+ Bands)
  • • GIC/Financial proof of living

VISITOR VISA

Benefits:

  • • Up to 10 years multi-entry (Canada)
  • • Explore global cultures effortlessly
  • • Attend seminars or visit family

Requirements:

  • • Strong home-country ties proof
  • • Clean criminal & medical history
  • • Purpose of travel & funds clarity

WORK VISA

Benefits:

  • • Path to Permanent Residency (PR)
  • • Earn in top global currencies
  • • Family sponsorship available

Requirements:

  • • Verified Job Offer (LMIA/Employer)
  • • Specific years of work experience
  • • PCC & Academic assessment (ECA)
